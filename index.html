Services

I will help you create positive changes, see new possibilities as well as assist you
in defining the steps needed to be taken to achieve both short term and long term goals.

The duration of a Coaching Session is about 60 minutes and the ideal regularity is weekly. One to one sessions can take place at the business premises of Coach Maria, via skype, face time or phone. There are Empowerment Coaching packages that have been created from the experience of Coach Maria Marczynski to provide a professional framework for cooperation, based on the uniqueness and preferences of the client. All packages can be modified upon the client’s individual needs and preferences.
