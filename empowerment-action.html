Empowerment Coaching in Action

Empowerment Coaching will help children and young people

Empowerment Coaching is a form of support that is proactive and change-based. Coaching uses tools and concepts from a range of other therapeutic models with a teaching and/or modelling element. Coaches help clients to:

  • Identify areas of their life they need help with
  • See issues from a different perspective
  • Choose their outcomes, responses and behaviours
  • Develop their life skills and resources to make those outcomes a reality

Coaching helps clients to understand their life experiences and challenges from two perspectives:

  • An external perspective: understanding the context in which their life is operating by exploring their behaviour and communication with others
  • An internal perspective: exploring and addressing thoughts, feelings, values, beleifs and needs

Coaching is built on the premise that we cannot change others, but we can change the way we relate to and react to others. Thus coaching can help people to positively deal with and manage their response to challenging situations in a positive manner in conjunction with having the principle that every problem has a solution. Every person is the expert on him/her self and has the power to determine their life.
