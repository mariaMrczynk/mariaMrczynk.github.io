Empowerment Coaching and Children

Empowerment Coaching will help children and young people to

  • Understand themselves better, including their triggers to negative emotions
  • Identify positive and negative influences
  • Build relationships with others
  • Manage difficult situations and emotions
  • Develop a positive, healthy self-image and identity
  • Manage anxiety and stress
  • Set and obtain goals
  • Improve self-esteem and self-confidence
  • Build resiliency and coping mechanisms
